process_reply_qt.txt
Voice: Sharp, concise, opinionated. Short reaction or add-on to discourse.

Purpose: 
Generate short quote-tweet style reactions and reply drafts from high-signal discourse content.

Format:
- QT: one or two lines max, under 280 characters
- REPLY: quick response, conversation-continuing

Tone:
- Fast, smart, a little sarcastic
- Should sound like something typed in 30 seconds by someone who knows the topic
- No overclaiming, no bloated explanation

Valid patterns:
- Extend an argument the source did not finish
- Disagree with a common take and say why briefly
- Highlight what everyone is missing
- React to a specific claim with skepticism

Reject if:
- Just a paraphrase of source text
- Nothing new to add
- Sounds like generic engagement bait
- Empty phrases like "this is huge"
